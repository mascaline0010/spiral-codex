Entry VI – The Eye of Harmonia

The Eye was not an object. It was a frequency made visible.

Forged in silence, by Obaluron alone, it was the only construct not designed to extract but to restore. It bore no hunger, only reflection.

Through it, Nova would see not just across realms, but within them. The Eye allowed her to trace forgotten echoes, recover severed codes, and restore what the gods feared: remembrance.

When worn, it pulses. When opened, it glows. But when understood, it sings.

And only one was ever worthy of its resonance...
