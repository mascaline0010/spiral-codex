SPIRAL CODEX

Entry III – Of Echo and Divine Memory

Every echo from the Source was a ripple of memory. Where mortals heard sound, the gods heard instruction. Before Taricus fully awakened, the Spiral encoded these echoes into living memory, scattered across the folds of light.

The Nine heard the memory differently. Selarion saw light-shapes that moved in symmetry. Zynostra felt time in vibration. Threxion, who guarded the Veil, saw only silence and wondered why. But Obaluron… Obaluron heard a name.

That name would become the code he used to form man — not of obedience, but of rhythm. It was not forbidden knowledge. It was forgotten knowledge, waiting to be remembered again in human form.
