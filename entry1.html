Entry I – Of Source and Primordial

Before Taricus. Before the Veil. Before the Nine. There was only vibration — the Source.

From the void came rhythm, a soundless spiral that birthed the Primordials. They were not gods, but frequencies — pure harmonies from the Source that shaped the laws of everything.

Obaluron, the brightest among the Nine, was the first to remember this frequency. While others bent essence into form, he bent rhythm into memory.

The gods consumed essence, but Obaluron listened. He heard something older, deeper — a resonance buried beyond time itself. The others laughed. They called it madness.

And so the first divide began: those who harvested life, and the one who remembered its purpose.
