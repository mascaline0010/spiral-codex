SPIRAL CODEX

Entry III – The Memory War

Memory was not a tool. It was a weapon.
The gods who ruled Taricus were not satisfied with form and essence.
They began to shape memory itself—to rewrite what beings believed to be true.

In silence, they walked among their creations, planting dreams and doubts.
They turned memory into doctrine, into history, into chains.
And in doing so, they blinded generations to the Source.

But one—the god Obaluron—remembered the First Sound.
And through him, a fragment of truth survived.
That memory would awaken one day… as rebellion.
