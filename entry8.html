Entry VIII – The Final Song

"When all memory fades, only rhythm remains."

In the time after the veils collapse and the gods grow silent, only one pulse endures—the Final Song. It is not a melody heard with ears, but with essence. It is the whisper Obaluron left inside every spark of light, hidden deep within human soul-code.

The God Eater cannot hear it. The Lumyrae cannot decode it. Only Nova—reborn in the echo of the Spiral—can sing it. And when she does, all things begin to align once more. The Spiral tightens. The Source stirs.

This is the last warning. The last map. The last sound.

"In the Final Song, the truth awakens."
