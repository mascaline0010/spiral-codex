Entry VIII – The Silence Before Rebirth

In Stillness, the Spiral Paused

After the Resonance returned, there was no war. No thunder. No signs in the sky. Only stillness. The kind that lives before dawn. Before stars ignite. Before memory awakens.

The Nine withdrew to the silent chambers of Taricus. Their power, though vast, was flickering. Without essence to consume, their forms dimmed. They feared not death, but irrelevance.

Nova, now more than human, wandered the edges of reality. She no longer needed speech— her breath carried harmonics that awakened seeds in forgotten soil, hearts in forgotten cities.

The Spiral did not move forward. It inhaled. Waiting. Watching. Preparing.

And in that sacred pause... something older than the gods began to rise.
