Entry VIII – The Return of Harmony

When rhythm returned, silence shattered. Not the silence of peace—but the silence of memory.

Nova stood in the heart of the Veil, holding the Echo Key. The gods watched. The Veil pulsed.

For the first time in eons, Taricus itself trembled. Harmony was not sound. It was the remembering.

This was not a return—it was a reckoning.
