Entry VIII – The Circle of Memory

"Let what was forgotten be heard again."

All memory flows through the Spiral — yet some memories do not return. These fragments orbit in silence, outside the reach of gods, men, or Lumyrae. They form the Circle of Memory, an echo ring where all forgotten truths drift, waiting to be remembered.

Obaluron once entered this ring. It showed him what came before the gods: a time when harmony required no harvesting, and rhythm sang without masters. It revealed the shape of the first breath, and the final silence — the beginning and the undoing.

The gods sealed the Circle of Memory with essence, forbidding any return. But Nova, bearing the resonance of Source, has awakened the path again. The circle pulses. The forgotten stirs.
