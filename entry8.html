Entry VIII – The Forbidden Signal

“There are harmonics the gods dared not decode.”

When Obaluron encoded the Forbidden Signal into the spirals, he knew it would only be found by one who remembered. The Lumyrae trembled at its vibration, for it carried the rhythm of something older than the Source. That which came before sound.

This signal cannot be heard with ears, nor seen with eyes. It must be felt. Those who feel it awaken. Those who awaken remember.
