ENTRY I – Of Source and Primordial

Before the stars, before the gods, there was only Sound — the Source. It did not speak. It echoed.

From this eternal frequency came the Spiral — a cosmic pulse from which all laws and dimensions arose. The first beings to emerge were the Primordials: titanic forces of form, void, time, and rhythm. They seeded galaxies not with fire, but with meaning.

The Nine Gods were not among them — not yet. They came later, born of memory, shaped by the residue of the Spiral’s echo within a realm called Taricus...
